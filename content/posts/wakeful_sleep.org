#+begin_example
As long as I sleep
  under the Veil of Ignorance,
I get dissolved into a state
  where I rest with desires
Half awake I project Dream
  Awake I project Illusion
Oh what Adventure
  it is to take
To the state of Wakeful sleep
  from the sleep of desires
#+end_example
