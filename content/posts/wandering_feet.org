#+begin_example
Going through
  an illusory maze
Colorful walls
  and, lots to play
Some paths are blocked
  Some go deeper
They keep me enchanted
  to hold me down
Leaving the ephemeral
  I walk with laughter
In search of infinite fields
  My feet wander
#+end_example
