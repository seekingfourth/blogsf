#+begin_example
That Supreme Infinite
    Illumines my mind
My mind is inert
    That breathes in life
Expressions I live
    Memories I scroll
Imaginations I weave
    Filtered through Desires
How Ignorant I am
    I take me as my mind
#+end_example
