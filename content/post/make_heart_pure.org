#+begin_example
World offered food
    To cater my desires
I went after it
    To have it all
Got exhausted
    and sick, I fell
The trick of desires
    Played me well
Now, I take it as meds
    Till the disease I cure
In Detachment and Love
    I make my heart pure
#+end_example
