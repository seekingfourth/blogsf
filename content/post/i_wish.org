#+begin_example
Sometimes I wish
  I could wander afar
But no destiny awaits
  to lead me
Sometimes I wish
  my ambitions could rise
But no desire erupts
  to inflame me
Sometimes I wish
  I could fall in love
But Love is all
  That claims me
#+end_example
