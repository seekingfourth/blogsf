#+begin_example
Drunk with desires
  Deep I sleep
Wandering in dream
  Laugh and weep
A voice I hear
  A soundless sound
To wake me up
  As an alarm
Ignorant I am
  To pay no heed
And, what for
  To snore more!
#+end_example
